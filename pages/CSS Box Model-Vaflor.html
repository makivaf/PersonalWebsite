RED

Red is often associated with intense emotions, particularly love and passion. It is a popular color for Valentine's Day and is commonly used to symbolize romantic love.

YELLOW

Yellow is often associated with positive emotions such as joy, happiness, and optimism. It is a bright and cheerful color that can evoke a sense of warmth and positivity.

BLUE

Blue is often associated with a sense of calm, tranquility, and peace. It has a calming effect on the mind and is frequently used in environments where a serene atmosphere is desired.

RAINBOW

The rainbow's diverse spectrum of colors coming together is often used as a symbol of unity, diversity, and inclusivity. It has been adopted as a symbol for LGBTQ+ pride and represents the diversity of the community.
